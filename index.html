"The Unknown" is a short story written by Nirab Paudel. This story is a complete work of fiction. Names, characters, places and incidents either are products of the author’s imagination or are used fictitiously. Any resemblance to actual events or locales or persons, living or dead, is entirely coincidental.

Alex Smith is a young guy from Vegas whose life changes dramatically after he witnesses something frightening. Follow Alex along his journey through anomalies while he attempts to explore the unknown.
